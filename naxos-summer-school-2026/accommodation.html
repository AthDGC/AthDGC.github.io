Accommodation

Free Municipality Dormitory

Location: Vivlos (Tripodes village), 8 km from Naxos Town (15 minutes by bus)

Available to: University (BA, MA, PhD) students who are participants of the Naxos Summer School and are not receiving a CIVIS mobility grant

Capacity: 40 beds - first-come, first-served

What to Bring

  • Your own bedsheets
  • Towels
  • Personal toiletries

Important Notes

  • No air conditioning available
  • No stable WiFi connection
  • Participants are responsible for keeping the premises clean
  • The organizing committee is not responsible for transportation or daily expenses of hostel participants
  • Bus service connects Vivlos to Naxos Town regularly

The Municipality of Naxos and Small Cyclades generously provides this hostel. Apply early as spaces are limited!

Other Accommodation Options

Traditional guesthouses, rooms and apartments for rent, and youth hostels are available near the venue in Naxos Town (Chora).

Many participants choose to stay in nearby beach areas such as:

  • Agios Prokopios
  • Agia Anna
  • Agios Georgios

These areas are connected to Naxos Town by frequent bus service (KTEL) and offer beautiful beaches alongside accommodation options.

Booking Tip: Book early for best availability and rates during the peak summer season (July). Apartments and studios are available for longer stays at competitive prices.
