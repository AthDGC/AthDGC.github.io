About AthDGC

The Athens Digital Glossa Chronos (AthDGC) Research Network is a leading center for corpus-based research on diachronic linguistics and language evolution. Based at the National and Kapodistrian University of Athens, we bring together researchers, students, and institutions to advance the understanding of language change through computational methods and digital humanities approaches.
