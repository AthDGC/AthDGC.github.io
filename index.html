About the Project

The Athens Digital Glossa Chronos (AthDGC) project develops corpus-based valency lexicons for ancient and medieval languages using innovative computational methods integrated with traditional philological expertise.

Funded by the Hellenic Foundation for Research and Innovation (HFRI) at the National and Kapodistrian University of Athens.
