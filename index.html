GLOSSA Contact

Journal of Language Contact

Peer-reviewed open-access journal and part of the family of GLOSSA journals dedicated to research on language contact phenomena, structural borrowing, contact-induced change, multilingualism, written language contact, translation studies, and areal linguistics.

Co-Editors-in-Chief: Nikolaos Lavidas, Ioanna Sitaridou, Igor Yanovich • ISSN: 2653-9519 • Open Access Publication

Research Areas:

  • Language contact and structural borrowing
  • Contact-induced language change
  • Multilingualism and code-switching
  • Written language contact through translations
  • Areal linguistics and linguistic diffusion
