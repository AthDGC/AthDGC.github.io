CVL-CDSAML Project

Corpus-based Valency Lexicon for a Contrastive and Diachronic Study of Ancient & Medieval Languages

This research project aims to develop a comprehensive valency lexicon that enables contrastive and diachronic studies of languages from antiquity to the present day. By leveraging corpus-based methodologies and computational linguistics approaches, we seek to uncover patterns of language change and evolution across different time periods and linguistic contexts.

Corpus Development

Building comprehensive digital corpora for ancient and medieval languages

Tracking language change patterns across centuries of linguistic evolution
