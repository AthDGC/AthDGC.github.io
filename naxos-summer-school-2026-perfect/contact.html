Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about the summer school

When do applications open?

Applications for the 2026 summer school will open on December 1, 2025. Early bird registration is available until February 28, 2026.

Who can apply to the summer school?

We welcome applications from graduate students (MA and PhD), early career researchers, advanced undergraduates, and professionals in relevant fields.

How many ECTS credits are awarded?

Participants who successfully complete the program will receive 6 ECTS credits, recognized across European universities.

Are scholarships available?

Yes, limited scholarships are available for students from underrepresented regions and those with financial need. Applications are due by February 15, 2026.

What is included in the registration fee?

The fee includes all sessions and materials, 6 ECTS credits, welcome and gala dinners, daily coffee breaks, certificate of completion, and social events.

How do I get to Naxos?

You can fly to Naxos Island National Airport (JNX) or take a ferry from Athens (Piraeus or Rafina ports). Regular ferries and flights connect Naxos with Athens.
