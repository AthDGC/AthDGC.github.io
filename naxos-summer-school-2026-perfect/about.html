About the Summer School

11 Years of Excellence in Diachronic Linguistics Education

Our Mission

Advancing the study of language change through exceptional education

The Naxos Summer School on Diachronic Linguistics brings together leading scholars and graduate students from around the world to explore the fascinating field of historical linguistics. Our mission is to provide intensive, high-quality training in diachronic linguistics while fostering international collaboration and cutting-edge research.

Since our inception in 2016, we have established ourselves as the premier international summer school for diachronic linguistics, attracting participants from over 30 countries and partnering with world-renowned academic institutions.
