Core Topics

Explore the fascinating field of diachronic linguistics

01

The Diachrony of English

Trace the evolution of English from Old English through Middle English to Modern English, examining phonological, morphological, and syntactic changes.

02

Ancient & Medieval IE Languages

Study innovative approaches to Indo-European languages using computational methods and digital humanities.

03

The Diachrony of Romance

Investigate the development of Romance languages from Latin through comparative analysis and language contact.

04

Comparative Diachronic Linguistics

Learn methodologies for comparing language change across different families and identifying universal patterns.

05

Language Contact

Explore how languages influence each other through contact, including borrowing and contact languages.

06

Research Workshop

Present your research and receive feedback from international experts in diachronic linguistics.
