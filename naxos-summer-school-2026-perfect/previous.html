Evolution of Themes

How our focus has evolved over the years

2016-2018

Foundational Period

Established core concepts in diachronic linguistics, grammaticalization, and language change.

2019-2021

Specialization Era

Focused on specific language families and computational approaches, including the transition to online formats.

2022-2025

Comparative Focus

Emphasis on comparative methodologies, language contact, and interdisciplinary approaches.
