Program Overview

A comprehensive six-day intensive program in diachronic linguistics

The 11th Naxos Summer School features an intensive six-day program combining theoretical foundations, practical methodologies, and current research in diachronic linguistics. Each day focuses on specific themes while maintaining interdisciplinary connections throughout.

Participants will engage in lectures, workshops, student presentations, and cultural activities, earning 6 ECTS credits upon successful completion.
