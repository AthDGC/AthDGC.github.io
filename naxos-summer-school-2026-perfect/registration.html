What's Included

Comprehensive package for your summer school experience

Academic Program

Full access to all lectures, workshops, and sessions with world-class faculty

Course Materials

All readings, digital resources, and course materials provided

Meals

Welcome dinner, gala dinner, and daily coffee breaks

Credits

6 ECTS credits upon successful completion

Certificate

Official certificate of participation and completion

Networking

Access to all social events and networking opportunities

What's Not Included

Items you'll need to arrange separately

  • Accommodation (see accommodation page for recommended options)
  • Travel expenses to/from Naxos
  • Most meals (except specified dinners and coffee breaks)
  • Personal expenses and travel insurance
  • Optional excursions and activities

Eligibility

Who can apply for the Naxos Summer School

Graduate Students

MA and PhD students in linguistics, philology, or related fields

Early Career Researchers

Postdocs and junior faculty within 5 years of PhD completion

Advanced Undergraduates

Exceptional undergraduate students with relevant background

Professionals

Researchers and practitioners in relevant fields

Application Process

Step-by-step guide to applying for the summer school

1

Prepare Documents

  • CV/resume (max 2 pages)
  • Statement of purpose (500 words)
  • Academic transcript (unofficial acceptable)
  • Letter of recommendation (optional but recommended)
2

Complete Application

Fill out the online application form with your personal and academic details

3

Submit Documents

Upload all required documents through the application portal

4

Await Decision

Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis. You'll receive a decision within 2 weeks.

5

Accept and Pay

Accept your offer and complete payment to secure your place
