Accommodation

Comfortable Stays in Beautiful Naxos

Where to Stay

We recommend several accommodation options in Naxos Town, all within walking distance of the summer school venue. Early booking is recommended as July is peak season.

Recommended Hotels

  • Swimming pool and restaurant
  • Air conditioning and WiFi
  • Continental breakfast included
  • €80-120 per night

Alternative Accommodation

Airbnb

Various apartments and rooms available throughout Naxos Town. Prices range from €50-150 per night depending on size and location.

Booking.com

Wide selection of hotels, apartments, and guesthouses. Filter by location to stay near the venue.

Local Guesthouses

Family-run guesthouses offering authentic Greek hospitality. Often more affordable than hotels.

Booking Tips

Book Early

July is peak tourist season. Book by March 2026 for best availability and prices.

Check Location

Ensure your accommodation is within walking distance of Naxos Town and the venue.

Air Conditioning

July can be hot. Ensure your accommodation has air conditioning.

Free Cancellation

Book with free cancellation in case your plans change.

What to Expect

  • Most accommodations include WiFi and air conditioning
  • Breakfast is often included in hotel rates
  • Many hotels have swimming pools
  • All are within easy walking distance of restaurants and shops
  • The venue is accessible from all recommended locations

Need Help?

If you need assistance with accommodation or have questions about where to stay, please contact us. We can provide recommendations based on your budget and preferences.
