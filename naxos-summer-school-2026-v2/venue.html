Venue

Naxos, Cyclades, Greece - The Perfect Setting for Learning

Location

The 11th Naxos Summer School on Diachronic Linguistics will be held in the beautiful island of Naxos, Greece. Known for its stunning beaches, ancient history, and vibrant culture, Naxos provides the perfect backdrop for intensive academic study and cultural exploration.

Main Venue

Naxos Cultural Center

Located in the heart of Naxos Town, our main venue offers modern facilities with traditional Cycladic architecture. The center provides:

  • State-of-the-art lecture halls
  • High-speed internet access
  • Comfortable seating and climate control
  • Beautiful sea views
  • Easy access to accommodations and restaurants

Facilities

Lecture Halls

Modern auditoriums with audio-visual equipment, perfect for lectures and presentations

Workshop Rooms

Smaller rooms designed for interactive workshops and group discussions

Study Areas

Quiet spaces for individual study and collaborative work

Coffee Break Areas

Comfortable spaces for networking and refreshments between sessions

Getting to Naxos

By Air

Fly to Naxos Island National Airport (JNX) with connections from Athens and other Greek islands. Alternatively, fly to Athens International Airport (ATH) and take a connecting flight or ferry.

By Ferry

Regular ferries connect Naxos with Athens (Piraeus and Rafina ports). The journey takes 4-6 hours depending on the ferry type. High-speed ferries are also available.

Local Transport

Naxos Town is easily walkable. Local buses and taxis are available for exploring the island. Car rentals are also an option for greater flexibility.

Climate

July in Naxos is warm and sunny, with average temperatures ranging from 24-28°C (75-82°F). The sea temperature is perfect for swimming, and the island enjoys plenty of sunshine with minimal rainfall.

Accessibility

The main venue is wheelchair accessible, and we strive to accommodate all participants with special needs. Please contact us in advance to discuss any specific requirements.

Local Amenities

Restaurants

Naxos offers excellent traditional Greek cuisine and international dining options

Shopping

Local shops, supermarkets, and boutiques within walking distance

Beaches

Beautiful beaches just a short walk from the venue

Historical Sites

Ancient temples, medieval castles, and archaeological museums nearby

Why Naxos?

Naxos provides the ideal environment for academic focus and cultural enrichment. The island's rich history dating back to ancient times, combined with its natural beauty and welcoming atmosphere, creates an inspiring setting for studying language change and historical linguistics.
