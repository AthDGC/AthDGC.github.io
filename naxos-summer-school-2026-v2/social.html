Social Program

Connect, Network, and Enjoy Naxos

Social Events

Our summer school includes a rich social program designed to foster connections, cultural exchange, and memorable experiences beyond the classroom.

Welcome Reception

Day 1 - July 19, 18:00-20:00

Meet and Greet

Join us for a welcome reception at the Naxos Cultural Center terrace overlooking the Aegean Sea. Meet fellow participants, faculty, and staff in a relaxed atmosphere.

  • Greek meze and refreshments
  • Introduction to the summer school
  • Networking opportunities
  • Live traditional music

Welcome Dinner

Day 1 - July 19, 20:00-22:00

Traditional Greek Dinner

Experience authentic Greek cuisine at a seaside taverna in Naxos Town. Enjoy traditional dishes, local wine, and the company of new colleagues.

  • Multi-course Greek dinner
  • Local wine and ouzo
  • Traditional music and dancing
  • Beachfront setting

Evening Workshops

Day 2 - Research Presentations

Share your research in progress and receive feedback from international experts and peers.

Day 3 - Methodology Workshop

Hands-on workshop on computational methods in historical linguistics.

Day 4 - Poster Session

Present your research posters and discuss projects with faculty and participants.

Island Excursion

Day 5 - July 23, Afternoon

Discover Ancient Naxos

Join us for a guided tour of Naxos's most important historical sites and traditional villages.

  • Visit Portara and ancient temples
  • Explore medieval Kastro
  • Tour traditional mountain villages
  • Swim at beautiful beaches
  • Traditional lunch at local taverna

Gala Dinner

Day 5 - July 23, 19:00-21:00

Farewell Celebration

Celebrate the conclusion of our summer school with a gala dinner at a premium restaurant overlooking the port.

  • Gourmet Greek cuisine
  • Wine pairing
  • Awards and certificates
  • Live music and dancing
  • Sunset views

Closing Lunch

Day 6 - July 24, 12:30-14:00

Farewell Gathering

Join us for a final lunch together before departure, sharing memories and planning future collaborations.

  • Buffet lunch
  • Certificate ceremony
  • Group photos
  • Future collaboration planning

Informal Gatherings

Coffee Breaks

Daily coffee breaks provide opportunities for informal discussions and networking.

Evening Strolls

Explore Naxos Town together in the evenings, discovering hidden gems and local cafes.

Beach Time

Free time for swimming and relaxation at nearby beaches during breaks.

Study Groups

Form study groups to discuss course material and work on assignments together.

Cultural Experiences

Traditional Music

Experience live Greek music and learn traditional dances.

Local Cuisine

Taste authentic Naxian dishes and learn about Greek gastronomy.

Historical Tours

Discover the rich history of Naxos through guided tours.

Local Crafts

Visit local artisans and learn about traditional crafts.

Networking Opportunities

The social program is designed to maximize networking and collaboration opportunities:

  • Mix with faculty from leading universities worldwide
  • Connect with graduate students from diverse backgrounds
  • Discuss research ideas and potential collaborations
  • Build international academic networks
  • Share experiences and best practices

Dress Code

  • Welcome events: Smart casual
  • Academic sessions: Business casual
  • Gala dinner: Formal/cocktail attire
  • Excursions: Casual and comfortable
  • Beach activities: Beachwear

Dietary Requirements

Please inform us of any dietary restrictions or allergies when registering. We accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other dietary needs.
