Help Advance Diachronic Linguistics Education

Support the Summer School

The Naxos Summer School on Diachronic Linguistics relies on the generous support of institutions, organizations, and individuals who share our commitment to advancing education and research in historical linguistics.

Why Support Us?

Academic Excellence

Support world-class education in diachronic linguistics and help train the next generation of scholars.

International Collaboration

Foster global connections between researchers and students from diverse backgrounds and institutions.

Research Advancement

Contribute to cutting-edge research in historical linguistics and language change.

Cultural Exchange

Support cultural and academic exchange in the beautiful setting of Naxos, Greece.

Ways to Support

Institutional Partnership

Universities and research institutions can partner with us through:

  • Faculty participation and exchange
  • Student scholarships and fellowships
  • Research collaboration opportunities
  • Joint programming and events
  • Resource sharing and expertise

Corporate Sponsorship

Companies and organizations can support through:

  • Event sponsorship
  • Technology and equipment support
  • Publication and dissemination support
  • Branding and visibility opportunities
  • Employee participation programs

Individual Donations

Individuals can contribute through:

  • Student scholarships
  • Book and resource donations
  • Volunteer expertise and time
  • General operating support
  • Endowment contributions
