About the Summer School

11 Years of Excellence in Diachronic Linguistics

Our Mission

The Naxos Summer School on Diachronic Linguistics brings together leading scholars and graduate students from around the world to explore the fascinating field of historical linguistics. Our mission is to provide intensive, high-quality training in diachronic linguistics while fostering international collaboration and research.

What Makes Us Special

Expert Faculty

Learn from world-renowned scholars in diachronic linguistics

Intensive Learning

6 days of focused study with 6 ECTS credits

Beautiful Setting

Study in the stunning island of Naxos, Greece

Global Community

Connect with researchers from over 30 countries

Academic Excellence

Our summer school is recognized internationally for its academic rigor and contribution to the field of diachronic linguistics. Participants receive comprehensive training in theoretical frameworks, methodologies, and current research trends in historical linguistics.

Part of CIVIS Alliance

As part of the CIVIS European University Alliance, we bring together expertise from leading European universities to create a unique learning environment that transcends traditional academic boundaries.
