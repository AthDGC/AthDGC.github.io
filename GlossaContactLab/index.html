About the Laboratory

Biblical Translation Analysis

Examining language contact effects in the Greek Septuagint and early biblical translations

Medieval Translation Studies

Investigating contact phenomena in medieval literary and religious translations

Digital Corpus Research

Applying computational methods to historical language contact analysis

The GlossaContact Laboratory specializes in the study of language contact phenomena through translations, with particular emphasis on ancient and medieval written language contact.

Based at the National and Kapodistrian University of Athens, the laboratory investigates how languages influence each other through translation practices, biblical and literary retranslations, and written language borrowing across historical periods.
