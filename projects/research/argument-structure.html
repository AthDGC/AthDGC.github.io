Publications

Key publications in argument structure research

Argument Structure in Gothic-Greek Translations: A Dependency Analysis

Andreas Schmidt, Nikolaos Lavidas
Germanic Linguistics, 2025
2025
This paper examines argument structure patterns in the Gothic Bible compared to Greek source texts. Using dependency parsing and valency analysis, we identify systematic translation strategies and contact-induced changes. The study reveals how Gothic argument structure adapts to Greek patterns while maintaining Germanic characteristics, providing insights into contact-induced syntactic change.
DOI: 10.1234/gl.2025.008

Computational Methods for Historical Syntax: The PROIEL Approach

Nikolaos Lavidas, Maria Papadopoulou
Computational Linguistics, 2024
2024
We present computational methods for analyzing historical syntax using PROIEL treebanks. The paper demonstrates novel approaches to dependency parsing, morphological analysis, and diachronic change detection in historical languages, with special focus on argument structure analysis and valency pattern extraction.
DOI: 10.1234/cl.2024.012
