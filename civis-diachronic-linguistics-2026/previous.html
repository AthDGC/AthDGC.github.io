Languages in Europe and their Diachronies

The fifth edition of our CIVIS Blended Intensive Programme, continuing the tradition of "Languages in Europe and their Diachronies" with enhanced focus on computational methods and digital humanities integration.

April-July 2025
Online + Naxos, Greece
6 ECTS Credits
11 CIVIS Universities

Program Structure:

  • Virtual Part (April 7 - June 30, 2025): Online spring school and workshops
  • Spring School (April 7-11, 2025): Intensive classes on linguistic and computational historical linguistics
  • Online Workshops (May-June 2025): Small computational projects "From texts to grammar"
  • Physical Mobility (July 21-25, 2025): Summer school in Naxos, Greece

Key Topics:

  • Computational historical linguistic analyses
  • New methodologies for describing language change
  • Modern theoretical approaches to ancient and medieval languages
  • Digital heritage data integration
  • Statistical analysis of Indo-European languages

Building on a Strong Foundation

While 2025 marked our fifth formal CIVIS Blended Intensive Programme, the initiative builds upon years of collaboration between CIVIS partner institutions in the field of diachronic linguistics. Our programme draws from:

Naxos Summer School Legacy

Over a decade of excellence in diachronic linguistics education through the internationally recognized Naxos Summer School series (2016-2026).

CIVIS Collaboration

Established partnerships between linguistics departments across the CIVIS alliance, fostering joint research and educational initiatives.

Digital Innovation

Pioneering work in computational historical linguistics and digital humanities, integrating cutting-edge technology with traditional philological methods.

Looking Ahead

Building on the success of our inaugural edition, the 2026 programme expands to include more CIVIS partner universities and enhanced computational components, continuing our mission to train the next generation of diachronic linguists.

Expanded Network

Growing from 8 to 11 CIVIS universities

Advanced AI Integration

Enhanced machine learning and NLP components

Global Impact

International collaboration and knowledge exchange
