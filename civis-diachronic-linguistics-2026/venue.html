Summer School Venue

Naxos, Cyclades, Greece | 20-24 July 2026

Photo: Naxos Island, Cyclades, Greece

Discover Naxos

Where Digital Innovation Meets Mediterranean Inspiration

Complete your interdisciplinary journey with five transformative days on the stunning island of Naxos in the Cyclades archipelago. Ancient heritage provides the perfect backdrop for cutting-edge computational linguistics.

Naxos is the largest island in the Cyclades, offering a unique blend of:

  • Rich historical and linguistic heritage dating back to ancient Greek civilization
  • Beautiful Mediterranean landscapes and beaches
  • Charming traditional villages and modern facilities
  • Excellent infrastructure for academic work
  • Inspiring environment conducive to learning and networking
Why Naxos? Experience the perfect fusion of historical depth and technological innovation, where the island's rich linguistic heritage enhances your understanding of language evolution through the ages.

Key Features of Naxos:

  • Rich historical and archaeological sites
  • Beautiful traditional Cycladic architecture
  • Stunning Mediterranean landscapes

Getting to Naxos

Naxos is accessible by both air and sea, with regular connections from Athens and other Greek islands.

By Air

  • Naxos Island National Airport (JNX): Direct flights from Athens (approximately 45 minutes)
  • Several daily flights operated by Olympic Air and Sky Express
  • The airport is located about 3 km from Naxos Town (Chora)

By Ferry

  • From Piraeus Port (Athens): Regular ferry services (3.5-6 hours depending on ferry type)
  • High-speed ferries: Faster but more expensive option
  • Conventional ferries: Slower but more economical
  • Multiple daily departures in summer
Travel Covered by Grant: The Erasmus+ mobility grant helps cover your travel expenses to and from Naxos. Detailed travel arrangements and reimbursement procedures will be provided to accepted students.

What to Expect in July

Weather

July in Naxos offers ideal summer weather:

  • Temperature: Average highs of 27-29°C (81-84°F)
  • Sunshine: Abundant sunshine with minimal rainfall
  • Sea temperature: Warm and perfect for swimming (24-25°C)
  • Conditions: Generally dry with a pleasant breeze

What to Pack

  • Light, comfortable clothing for warm weather
  • Sunscreen and sun hat
  • Laptop and any required study materials
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Swimsuit (for free time)
  • Light jacket for air-conditioned rooms
  • Power adapter (EU plugs, 220V)
